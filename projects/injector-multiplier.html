CASE STUDY

Injector Multiplier

Product developed
Overview

A 1-input to 4-output injector driver for engine applications.

The Problem

Driving four injectors from a single signal at 12 V and 8 A peak per channel is a power-electronics and protection problem: switching speed, flyback handling, and channel isolation.

System Architecture

Four cleanly switching driver channels with protection design, packaged as a product.

My Role & What I Built

Power-stage design, protection circuitry, board design, and validation.

Tech Stack
  • 12 V / 8 A per channel
  • Injector driving
  • Protection design
