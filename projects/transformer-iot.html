CASE STUDY

Industrial Transformer Monitoring System

POC for Indo Tech Transformers Ltd
Overview

Industrial IoT monitoring for power transformers, built as a proof of concept for Indo Tech Transformers.

The Problem

Transformer health depends on parameters — pressure, voltage, current, temperature, running hours — usually checked manually and intermittently.

System Architecture

PT100 temperature sensing, ZMPT101B voltage and SCT013 current measurement; data streamed to the cloud over HTTP/JSON and shown on a P10 LED panel on-site, with RTC-timestamped SD logging as offline fallback.

My Role & What I Built

Sensor integration, firmware, cloud pipeline, display driver, and the logging fallback.

Tech Stack
  • PT100
  • ZMPT101B
  • SCT013
  • RTC + SD
  • HTTP/JSON
  • P10 display
Outcome

POC delivered to Indo Tech Transformers Ltd.
